🧑‍🏫 How to Use It (Simple 2-Step Process)

The tool is a ready-to-use Google Sheet powered by Apps Script.

✅ Step 1: Fill in the EmailDraft Sheet

Fill in the following columns:

  • Recipient_Email: Recipient’s email address
  • Email_Subject: Subject line of the email
  • Body_RichText: The message body (supports rich formatting)
  • Image_URL: (Optional) Link to an image you want to include

You can format your message using the Google Sheets toolbar (bold, italic, colors, etc.) without needing to know HTML.

📤 Step 2: Send the Emails

From the top menu, go to 📧 Email Tools and select Send Emails. After you approve the permissions, a popup will confirm how many emails were sent. Sent emails will be marked with a timestamp to prevent duplicates.

To resend an email, simply clear the Send_Status and Sent_At columns for that entry and run the script again.
