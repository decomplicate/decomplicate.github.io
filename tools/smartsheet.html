✅ What is Smartsheet Guard?

Smartsheet Guard is a powerful extension that empowers you to lock down critical sections of your Smartsheet — without complex permissions or clunky workarounds.

🔍 How It Works

With Smartsheet Guard, you can define exactly which cells, rows, columns, or ranges are critical to your workflow. Whether it’s formulas, summaries, headers, or key inputs — you’re in control.

🔔 When someone tries to edit a protected area, they’re instantly shown a clear, customizable pop-up message alerting them that the section is locked.

🛡️ This simple interaction stops unintended changes before they happen, helping teams maintain clean, error-free data — especially in collaborative environments.

💼 Key Use Cases

  • Finance teams protecting formulas or totals
  • Project managers securing timeline fields
  • Admins maintaining template integrity
  • Collaborative teams avoiding accidental overwrites
  • Shared sheets where critical data must remain untouched
